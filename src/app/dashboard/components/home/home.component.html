About Us

In numerous nations, individuals rely on agencies for skilled services, while elsewhere, tradespeople work independently, directly with clients. Our focus is on this craftsman segment and their community.


Before the app, challenges encompassed unstable employment, scarcity of reputable craftsmen, risks of engaging untrained workers, subpar services, exploitative pricing, absence of pre-agreed pricing, and safety concerns, such as employing individuals with criminal records.
