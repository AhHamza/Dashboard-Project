About Us

In many countries, people count on private agencies or companies to provide them with professional electricians, plumbers, carpenters or painters. On the other hand, there are still countries where craftsmen or tradesmen work as individuals with direct connection to their clients. We focus on this segment of craftsmen and the society around them.

Before creating this app there were several problems and some of these problems was that there were unstable craftsmanship jobs for workers, unavailability of the good reputation craftsmen, possibility of dealing with inexperienced and untrained craftsmen, low quality services, exploitation or unfairly high costs, lack of pricing agreement in advance, security issues (i.e. ordering craftsmen with criminal record).

The app provides

● It saves clients time by providing them with the craftsman sooner as possible.

● Provide more jobs to the workers, which lead to a stable income.

Reliable services

● Ratings and reviews will increase the service quality.

● The competitive environment will reduce costs.

Great support

● The app provides great support for the community
